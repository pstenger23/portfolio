Disc Score

I have been playing disc golf regularly for a couple of years
now and I have never been too good at keeping/remembering what
I was shooting on the round. The purpose is to keep track of
your score during a round of disc golf. I have found that keeping
score in disc golf is a little more difficult as at most courses
you are not provided with a score card, so I made a site that acts
as such. It works for any course, and any amount of holes, as the
tally can be infinite.
